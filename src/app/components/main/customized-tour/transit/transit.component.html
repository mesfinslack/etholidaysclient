Transit Tour

When you next have a transit stop in Addis Ababa, why not consider a City Tour and use the time to explore “The New Flower”!

Addis Ababa “New Flower” in Amharic is a vibrant cosmopolitan capital city and our City Tour will give you a real taste and flavour of Ethiopia. Highlight’s will include a visit to the National Museum, home of the world famous hominid Lucy and learn about her discovery in the Afar region of Ethiopia in 1974 plus (experience shopping on the Churchill Avenue around post office where you can find traditional scarfs, crosses and much more) You will of course also experience a traditional Ethiopian coffee ceremony from roasting to brewing and savouring the taste of the drink that originated in Ethiopia.

If you are in the mood for some relaxation and down time during your transit time we have you covered. The exclusive spa offer will help you to rejuvenate yourself in a welcoming and luxurious setting, pampering your body with a choice of relaxing massages, steam, sauna or body treatment.

Addis Ababa is the third highest capital in the world with an altitude of 2,400 m. Founded in 1887 by Emperor Menelik II, it is also considered the capital of Africa for hosting multiple international institutions including the African Union and the United Nations Economic Commission for Africa.

Travelers for business or pleasure enjoy Africa’s bustling and vibrant capital city. It has an ethnically diverse population of more than 4million and is known for hospitality and a readiness to help travelers create experiences that become long lasting memories.
