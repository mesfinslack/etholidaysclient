  • airplanemode_activeDay 2: Island Nosy Be Tour, east to west
    • Breakfast at the hotel.
    • Departure from the hotel at 08h00 This tour allows you to discover the inside of Nosy Be. Among other highlights, you’ll visit the remnants of the SIRAMA sugar factory in Dzamandzar. In the center of Nosy Be, Hellville boasts nice colonial buildings and a nice local market.
    • A little outside Hellville, you’ll be able to enjoy the sight of the sacred Banyan tree among the many perfumes of coffee, pepper, vanilla and Ylang Ylang along the way. You’ll end the tour with a visit of Mont Passot, the highest site in Nosy Be, from which you’ll be able to enjoy the sunset before going back to the hotel.

  • airplanemode_activeDay 3: Nosy Komba and Tanikely islands
    • Breakfast at the hotel
    • Departure at 08h00, will embark on a mini-cruise aboard a catamaran to enjoy the two slands near Nosy be. Nosy Komba, your first stop, is the second biggest island of the area behind Nosy Be. It’s a traditional fishermen’s village with a handicraft market, where one can shop for sculptures and traditional table clothes. Nosy Komba is also famous for its small Lemurs’ park. Afterwards, you’ll get to Nosy Tanikely (literally, « little land island »), a marine reserve that shelters all kinds of multi-colored fish, corals, anemones, and even sea turtles. Snorkeling (equipment provided) in Nosy Tanikely is a truly unique experience, as one feels as if inside a real life-size aquarium. The seafood picnic lunch will take place on the catamaran after the snorkeling session.
    • Picnic lunch during the excursion
