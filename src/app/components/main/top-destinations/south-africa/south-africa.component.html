South Africa

Welcome to a nation where you can go on safari and visit the beach without having to travel great distances. Tours in this country will take you to a variety of different locations that celebrate diversity in both culture and landscape. This nation is renowned for its wide open skies and welcoming locals, making it a popular destination all year round.

Botswana

Home of the greatest areas to explore African wildlife on land as well as in water. Whatever Botswana package you choose will leave you with a camera stocked full of exceptional photos and an experience unlike any other.

Zimbabwe

A nation of spectacular natural beauty, friendly people and rich culture, Zimbabwe’s status as one of Africa’s leading safari destinations is once again emerging as a vacation highlight of the continent. Victoria Falls – known to locals as ‘The Smoke That Thunders’ – is one of the seven natural wonders of the world and the sheer power of this massive body of water plunging into the Zambezi Gorge is awe-inspiring and unforgettable. Lake Kariba, with its game-rich shores and islands, is an idyllic safari spot featuring mind-blowing sunsets; Hwange National Park is known for its huge herds of elephants; and a kayak trip down the Zambezi through the Mana Pools National Park will appeal to the intrepid traveller, providing close encounters with crocodiles, hippos and a host of other wildlife.
