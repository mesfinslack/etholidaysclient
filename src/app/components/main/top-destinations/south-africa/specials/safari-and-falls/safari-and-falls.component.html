  • airplanemode_activeDay 1:Transfer airport- hotel

    Arrival at Victoria Falls International Airport, meet & greet by Ethiopian Holidays tour representative and transfer to your hotel for check in. This afternoon we enjoy a sunset Cruise on the Zambezi River, a few kilometers above the mighty Victoria Falls themselves. With binoculars and cameras at the ready we have a good chance of spotting hippo, crocodile, and various species of antelope as well as elephants that come down sunset with a test of Vic Falls Dinner. Overnight: Hotel.

    Meal Plan: Cruise Dinner

  • airplanemode_activeDay 2: The Amazing Victoria Falls

    Today we visit the spectacular and one of the seven Natural wonders of the world Victoria Falls, enjoy a guided tour of the Victoria Falls, also known by the local name of “Mosi-oa Tunya”(the smoke that thunders). The walking safari takes you through the magnificent rain forest to view the cataracts, Livingstone’s statue and a wide variety of birds and plants. The falls are massive and invite contemplation from many different viewpoints. You are invited to walk around the area and enjoy vistas. Raincoats are provided when needed. In the evening you will have Traditional Bomma Dinner.

    Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch

    Overnight: Hotel

  • airplanemode_activeDay 3: Victoria falls at Leisure

    Full day at leisure, spend the day exploring on your own pace

  • airplanemode_activeDay 4: Game Drive

    Today After breakfast you will be taken to Victoria Falls Private Game Reserve for half day game drive. Victoria Falls is a major hub for safaris onward into other African countries. Many wildlife adventures can be combined to create the ultimate safari beginning or ending with an awe inspiring view of one of the Seven Wonders of the World. The Victoria Falls Game Reserve is a Big Five safari destination; lion, elephant, buffalo, leopard and rhino can all be seen. The savanna is full of wildlife and apart from the big 5, giraffe, waterbuck, zebra and sable all regularly roam across game-vehicle paths.

    Meal Plan: Breakfast

  • airplanemode_activeDay 5: Return

    After breakfast, enjoying your free morning at leisure or explore Victoria Falls Local market until transfer to the airport. Check out from hotel at noon and transfer to airport.

    Meal Plan: Breakfast
