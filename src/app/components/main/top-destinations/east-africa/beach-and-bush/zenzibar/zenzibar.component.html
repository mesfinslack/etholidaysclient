  • airplanemode_activeDay 3: Stone Town Tour and Spice tour

      After your breakfast at the hotel you will be picked for half day Stone Town city tour. On this tour you will trace he steps of the past through the winding alleys. Traders stack their baskets and the neat piles of fruit to sell. Children play in the streets in residential areas; mamas sit out and gossip while the men play board game. Get to know the distinct personality of stone town. You will have time to shop before your lunch in the city After lunch you will be taken on spice tour, Touch, smell and taste different tropical fruits. Learn about their properties, their origins and their medicinal and culinary uses. A visit to a spice farm village gives you insight into local life and the opportunity to try some Swahili dishes; taste the fruits in season and spice tea. Depending on the season, expect to find vanilla, cardamom, cinnamon, nutmeg, turmeric, lemon grass, cloves, ylang ylang, cumin, garlic, ginger, coriander, pepper, allspice, tamarind, chili, oregano and more.
