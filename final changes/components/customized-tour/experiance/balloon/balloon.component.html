Join us in the celebration of man’s earliest and surely most romantic form of flight – the hot air balloon. You will enjoy your balloon flight, which is tranquil, yet exhilarating. As the world slowly awakens, you float peacefully overhead, witnessing the serenity in slow motion from our balloon basket.
After the landing, all participants receive a diploma and share in the two hundred year old tradition of a champagne toast. A celebration of both new found friends and the heavens above. The world seems an almost perfect place when viewed from the basket of a balloon. During a balloon flight you will not feel any sensation of heights because the balloon is detached from the ground. Also there is no feeling of turbulence whatsoever as the balloon drifts with the wind and we do not fly in weather conditions that are turbulent.
If the romance and adventure of balloon flight appeals to you, permit us to share with you the magic and excitement of a balloon flight.
